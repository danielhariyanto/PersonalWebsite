I want to share a project that I’m very passionate about. Mobile Memory is a MassChallenge startup I co-founded that aims to prevent Alzheimer’s via an accessible, non-invasive screening tool. We have developed a machine learning model that can take a user’s voice sample and predict whether that user will develop Alzheimer’s in the future.

The idea is inspired by the discovery that the region of the brain responsible for speech production is one of the first to be affected by dementia. Machine learning is able to pick up subtle cues in speech to predict the chance of developing Alzheimer’s before symptoms arise. Through lifestyle changes and proper medication, the user can delay the onset of the disease and even prevent it entirely.

We’re currently undergoing clinical validation in Sweden with the Carney Institute for Brain Science at Brown.

Link to website: https://www.mobilememory.app/
